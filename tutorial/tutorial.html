The Loop of Collective Learning

How Testify Operates

A completely democratized testing sandbox built for students, curated by students. Experience the seamless workflow below.

1

Democratized Polling

Enter the sandbox. Cast your votes on which Subject, Chapter, Target Pattern (JEE Mains or Advanced), Question Pool Size, and Time limits you want to practice next.

2

The 5-Hour Lock-In

When any 3 students cast initial votes on a set of parameters, a countdown timer of exactly 5 hours is triggered. Anyone else can jump in to vote and swing the final test profile before the lock-in.

3

Crowdsourced Uploads

Once parameters freeze, the sandbox opens up the upload window. Each student can directly contribute up to 5 target questions to build the custom exam paper.

4

Launch & Infinite Loop

The system generates, compiles, and deploys the test directly into the practice dashboard. Attempt it at your convenience (zero strict due dates), unlock full visual performance analytics, comparative rankings, and immediately start a new poll.
